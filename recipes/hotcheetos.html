How to make Hot Cheetos and Cheese

Ingredients

  • Nacho Cheese
  • Hot Cheetos
  • Optional: grilled chopped beef
  • Optional (if you're feeling fancy): chopped scallions

Steps

  1. Heat up that nacho cheese in a saucepan or microwave
  2. Pour that son of a b on straight into the hot cheeto bag
  3. Add toppings as you will
  4. Chow down like there's no tomorrow
